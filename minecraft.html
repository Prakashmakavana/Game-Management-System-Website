🟩 Minecraft

Category: Sandbox & Survival

🎮 About this game:

**Minecraft** is a sandbox survival game where players explore, build, and survive in a blocky 3D world.

With endless possibilities, create your own adventures, battle mobs, and craft tools to survive.

🌟 **Key Features:**

  • 🌍 Open-world exploration
  • 🛠️ Crafting & building
  • 👹 Survival mode with monsters
  • 🎮 Multiplayer & creative mode
